About The Doctor

Meet Dr. Sarah Mitchell

Expertise & Compassion Combined

Dr. Sarah Mitchell is a board-certified internal medicine physician with over 15 years of clinical experience. She completed her medical training at Harvard Medical School and her residency at Massachusetts General Hospital.

Dr. Mitchell believes in treating the whole person, not just the symptoms. She takes time to listen to her patients and develops personalized treatment plans that address both immediate health concerns and long-term wellness goals.
